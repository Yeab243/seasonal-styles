Outfitter for All Seasons!


The content changes based on the season, including a look, feel and logo. I used CSS style syntax with a jQuery shortcut to change styles. I used the switch statment to change items on the page to match the season. The click event handler in the jQuery finds all the a tags inside class of seasons. The page promotes a unique look and feel for each of the four seasons by changing the page in the 4 following areas:

  • A unique color scheme in the stylesheet or background color.
  • A unique logo of for every season.
  • A unique item of clothing.
  • A unique quote for every season.
